Destinations

Brazil!

A contrast of luscious green forests and bustling metropolitan cities. An enchanting land that never sleeps and where music is the soul of it's people. You can relax by a mesmerizing beach of white sand and crystal blue waters, or you can get your freak at one of the sparkling nightclubs. Or maybe you'd like to be chased by a black jaguar in the amazon rain-forest. Whatever your preference, there is never a dull moment when you visit Brazil.

Denmark!

A place best known as the world's happiest country. The birthplace of the mysterious conquerors, the Vikings, and consequently, abnormally large people (they ranked 4th country with the tallest population). Denmark is a land of fairy tales and it looks nothing short of something from famous Hans Christian Anderson's tales.

France!

A country renowned as one of the most romantic countries in the world, it inspires people to want to get married, not to mention gain a few pounds because come on, French pastry! France brings out the artist in it's tourists because of it's stunning artistic and cultural heritage. Prepared to be inspired, who knows maybe you'll even create something of your own.

Netherlands!

Most famous for it's capital, Amsterdam, windmills and tulips, you can rent a bike and cycle anywhere. With cyclist friendly roads, this is the favorable mode of transport, especially if you're a tourist. You get to see some pretty beautiful places while also pretending to be fit (that's what we did!). An awesome destination no matter the weather, so really, what's the excuse?
